Xiongmao Chinese Learning App

This design is for the Chinese practice app case study, Xiongmao. I mimicked the common style of these types of apps. Bright colors and playful themes are the standard.

The red color is the color of China's flag. The panda is China's national animal. Xiongmao mean "panda". Flat illustrations is the primary graphic style outside of vocabulary images.
