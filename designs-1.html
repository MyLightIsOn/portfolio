Envvia Shop

This is the consumer facing storefront for Envvia. This was designed in parallel with the admin side. This is an ongoing project and will feature our AI driven assistant in an upcoming update.

In this design, we wanted to go for a very "light" feel with very neutral, subdued colors. Because Envvia sells jewelry for other brands, they do not want their branding to overshadow their partner's branding.
